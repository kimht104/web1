The Wayback Machine is a digital time capsule created by the Internet Archive . It is maintained with the content of Alexa Internet . Called the "three-dimensional index", the archive allows users to view web pages that have been preserved over time. Preserved web pages are released 6 to 12 months after they are saved. This is because the storage cycle is different. We don't track the website for every update, but we store it every few weeks.
